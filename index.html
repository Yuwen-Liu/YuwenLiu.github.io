About Me

I received my M.Sc. degree from the School of Computer Science, Qufu Normal University in 2019, and I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. degree in the School of Computer Science and Technology, China University of Petroleum (East China), advised by Prof. Lianyong Qi. My research interests include point-of-interest recommendation, time series prediction.
